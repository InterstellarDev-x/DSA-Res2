## 2025 Trend Analysis

- **Dijkstra variants on the rise at Google & Amazon.** *Path With Minimum Effort* and *Swim in Rising Water* — both "minimize the maximum edge" minimax-Dijkstra problems — started appearing regularly, testing whether candidates can adapt the relax step rather than recite vanilla Dijkstra.
- **Union-Find surging at Amazon.** *Number of Islands II* (incremental) and continued *Accounts Merge* reflected a deliberate shift toward dynamic-connectivity reasoning over static traversal.
- **Number of Islands & Course Schedule remained universal.** Still the two most consistently asked questions across all five companies — the unchanging baseline.
- **Alien Dictionary held as a Google staple.** No sign of retirement; the prefix-conflict edge case continued to separate strong from average candidates.
- **Tarjan resurfaced at Google senior loops.** Critical Connections reasserted bridges/articulation as an L5 differentiator.
