## 2024 Trend Analysis

- **Fundamentals dominated.** Number of Islands and Course Schedule were the baseline expectation at nearly every company — if you knew only two graph problems, these were the two.
- **Traversal-first.** The bulk of 2024 questions were plain BFS/DFS and grid flood-fill; weighted shortest-path appeared mostly as a single Dijkstra (Network Delay Time) at the senior level.
- **Union-Find emerging at Amazon/Goldman.** Accounts Merge and connected-components questions signaled growing appetite for DSU, foreshadowing the 2025 rise.
- **Google stayed graph-build heavy.** Alien Dictionary and Word Ladder remained the signature "can you see the hidden graph" questions.
