## Emerging 2026 Trends

- **Find Median from Data Stream** is now the de facto Google L4 heap design question — appeared in every tracked Google loop in 2025-2026
- **IPO** is rising at Google as a "greedy + two heaps" combination problem at L4-L5
- **Sliding Window Median** (using TreeMap instead of PriorityQueue) is being asked at Google L5+ — tests knowledge of O(log n) deletion
- **Top K Frequent Elements** with the bucket sort O(n) optimization is being asked more at Amazon to distinguish strong candidates
