## 2024 trend analysis

- **Coin Change and House Robber were the universal OA staples** — both appeared at Amazon, Microsoft, and Goldman Sachs. If you prepped only two DP problems for 2024 screens, these were the two.
- **Edit Distance was firmly the Google DP signature**, and Microsoft adopted it onsite with a diff-tool framing.
- **Word Break began rising at Amazon** phone screens — the start of a multi-year climb.
- **Interval DP (Burst Balloons) was a Google L5 differentiator**, not seen at the easier-bar companies.
- **Stock problems were consistent** across companies but mostly at the easier (single transaction) end this year.
- **LIS appeared at the O(n²) level**; the O(n log n) follow-up was occasional, not yet expected by default.
