## 2025 trend analysis

- **Coin Change & House Robber stayed universal OA staples** — unchanged from [2024](./2024.md).
- **Word Break's rise at Amazon accelerated**, moving from phone-screen curiosity to a frequent OA item, often with the "return all sentences" (Word Break II) follow-up.
- **LIS O(n log n) became the expected answer at Google**, not just an optional upgrade — the O(n²) solution alone now reads as incomplete in a senior loop.
- **Stock problems shifted toward the multi-transaction / fee variants** at Goldman Sachs, exercising the full state machine rather than the single-transaction base.
- **Edit Distance remained the Google signature** and the Microsoft onsite mainstay.
- **Interval DP (Burst Balloons) stayed a Google L5 hard differentiator.**
- Microsoft leaned into the **obstacle / variant** versions (Unique Paths II) to test adaptation rather than recall.
