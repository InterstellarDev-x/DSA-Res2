# Greedy Algorithms — 2024 Interview Questions

## Overview
2024 saw a strong emphasis on interval scheduling and jump game problems across major tech companies, with Hard problems (Candy) appearing at Goldman Sachs and heap-based greedy (Task Scheduler) appearing at Amazon and Google.

## 2024 Key Themes

1. **Interval scheduling remained dominant** — 40% of questions across all companies involved interval sorting/merging.
2. **Heap-based greedy gaining traction** — Task Scheduler and Meeting Rooms II appeared more frequently at senior levels.
3. **Easy problems as warmups** — Lemonade Change and Assign Cookies appeared in phone screens and intern OAs to assess baseline greedy intuition.
4. **Goldman Sachs difficulty creep** — Hard problems like Candy started appearing in 2024 quantitative engineering roles.
