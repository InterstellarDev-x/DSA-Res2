## OA Overview

Goldman Sachs OA (HireVue / HackerRank): 2–3 coding problems in 60–70 minutes. Array problems often have a financial flavor (stock prices, portfolio allocation) but map to standard DSA patterns. GS tends to value correctness and edge cases over raw speed.
