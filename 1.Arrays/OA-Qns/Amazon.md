## OA Overview

Amazon OA typically consists of 2 coding problems in 90 minutes + 1–2 work simulation / debug sections. Array problems are common in both rounds. Recent trend: 2024–2026 OAs heavily feature sliding window, two pointers, and greedy patterns on arrays.
