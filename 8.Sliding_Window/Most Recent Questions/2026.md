## Emerging 2026 Trends

- **Minimum Window Substring** is the #1 must-know sliding window problem — appeared in every Amazon and Google interview loop tracked in 2025–2026
- The `atMost(k) - atMost(k-1)` technique for exact count problems is increasingly tested — expect at least one "count subarrays with exactly k" problem per loop
- **Character Replacement** is a rising Amazon OA question in 2026 — the `if` vs `while` shrink distinction is a key differentiator
- Two Pointers (3Sum, Container With Most Water) remain stable at mid-level; `4Sum` appears rarely
