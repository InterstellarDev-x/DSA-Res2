## Google Interview Style Notes

Google's recursion problems emphasize **proof of correctness**:
- N-Queens: Explain why `row - col` and `row + col` uniquely identify diagonals
- Expression Add Operators: Explain the `mult` variable and why it's necessary for `*` precedence
- Word Break II: Distinguish backtracking (all solutions) vs DP (just feasibility check)
