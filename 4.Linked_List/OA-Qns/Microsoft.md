## Microsoft Interview Notes

Microsoft tends toward **medium difficulty** LL problems with clean edge-case handling:
- Palindrome: Candidates who reverse-and-restore score better than those who use O(n) extra space
- Intersection: Must explain the two-pointer restart trick — brute force O(n²) is not accepted
- LRU Cache: Often appears in system design discussion format before full coding
