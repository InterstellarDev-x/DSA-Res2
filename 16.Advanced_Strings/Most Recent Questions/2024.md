## Trend Analysis — 2024

- **Longest Palindromic Substring** appeared at three of five companies — already the universal string staple.
- Google was the sole company testing **KMP-derived hard problems** (Shortest Palindrome, Longest Happy Prefix), establishing them as differentiators.
- `strStr` remained the canonical easy warm-up at Amazon and Microsoft, almost always allowing `indexOf` but rewarding a KMP explanation.
- Rolling-hash problems (Longest Duplicate Substring) were confined to Google senior loops.
